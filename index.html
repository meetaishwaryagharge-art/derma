Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our treatments and clinic

FAQ

Your Questions Answered

What skin conditions do you treat?
+
We treat a wide range of skin conditions including acne and acne scars, pigmentation, dark spots, melasma, warts, skin tags, infections, allergies, eczema, psoriasis, rosacea, and various chronic and infectious skin disorders. We also provide cosmetic dermatology and skin rejuvenation treatments.
Are aesthetic procedures safe?
+
Yes, all our aesthetic procedures are performed by qualified specialist Dr. Pradnya Asutkar using advanced, approved technology and medical-grade products. We follow strict hygiene and safety protocols to ensure patient safety at every step.
How long do treatments take?
+
Treatment duration varies by procedure. Consultations typically take 30–45 minutes. Most treatment sessions range from 30 minutes to 1.5 hours. A full treatment plan will be outlined during your initial consultation including session duration and estimated total timeline.
Is PRP treatment painful?
+
PRP treatment involves a mild level of discomfort during the injection phase. Topical numbing cream is applied beforehand to minimize discomfort. Most patients describe it as a mild pricking sensation. The procedure is well-tolerated by the majority of patients.
How many laser sessions are required?
+
The number of laser sessions depends on the treatment type and individual factors. Laser hair removal typically requires 6–8 sessions for permanent reduction. Pigmentation and scar treatments may need 3–6 sessions. Exact recommendations are provided during your consultation.
What precautions should be followed after treatment?
+
Post-treatment care instructions vary by procedure. General precautions include sun protection, avoiding harsh skincare products, adequate hydration, and following the specific aftercare guidelines provided by Dr. Asutkar. Detailed aftercare instructions are given after every treatment session.
Can men also undergo these treatments?
+
Absolutely. We provide comprehensive skin, hair, and aesthetic treatments for both men and women of all ages and skin types. Many of our male patients seek treatments for acne, hair fall, laser hair removal, and skin rejuvenation.
How soon are results visible?
+
Results vary depending on the treatment. Some procedures like Hydra Facial show immediate results. PRP therapy for hair growth shows gradual improvement over 3–6 months. Laser treatments for pigmentation may show significant results within 2–4 sessions. Your doctor will set realistic expectations during consultation.
Are consultations available before treatment?
+
Yes, every patient begins with a thorough initial consultation. Dr. Asutkar conducts a detailed skin/hair analysis, reviews your medical history, understands your concerns and goals, and recommends the most suitable personalized treatment plan before any procedure begins.
How do I book an appointment?
+
You can book an appointment by calling us at 8459323304, messaging us on WhatsApp, filling out the appointment form on our Contact page, or visiting the clinic directly at Plot No. 9/30, Besa Road, Manish Nagar, Nagpur. Our clinic is open Monday to Saturday, 10AM–2PM and 6PM–9PM.
